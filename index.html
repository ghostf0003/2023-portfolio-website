Shop and Contact

Currently, I am working on the creation of my own business. I hope to sell Kandi*, and my longer-term goal is to sell prints of my art! Since creating an online business can be difficult and tedious, I plan to sell from an Etsy Shop until I can make my own place. As of right now, my shop still needs to be fully set up; however, you can check back soon to view my Etsy shop.

I have my shop name set as SpectralCreationsCo, but this is subject to change.

*Kandi is typically described as a type of jewelry, amongst other things. Most of Kandi is created with plastic pony beads, stretchy elastic thread/chord, and sometimes charms. Examples of kandi creations include large and small bracelets- called cuffs and singles, respectively, decorative masks, necklaces, purses, and more!
